Welcome to FinWise
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FinWise is an easy-to-use personal finance management system made to assist people in monitoring, controlling, and enhancing their financial practices. It offers a wide range of tools that let users keep an eye on their earnings, outlays, savings, and associated bank accounts on a single, consolidated platform. The solution seeks to empower customers to get greater control of their finances through organized data, informative analytics, and smart budgeting features.
